FAQ
Q: What should my first goal be?
A: The story provides some clear cut goals that you can focus on, the first is to earn 10 coins.
If a story goal is taking a while, you can set some intermediate goals by aiming to complete achievements.
Story goals and achievements both often unlock new actions or features.

Q: Why do my coins decrease / why do I not get coins from certain actions?
A: Your character must each on a consistent basis. It costs him 1 coin every 4 seconds to fend off starvation.
This coin is occasionally subtracted from your purse at the same time as you get paid, which is why you see a missing payment.

1st of January in the year 600
You are a young man, down on his luck after his family home was raided by bandits. You have travelled from town to town, heading North as you go and finally you have reached the town of Bei Zhen. Several of the towns prior, you had only barely managed to scrape together enough money to buy some bread and water, enough to keep you moving. But this town, it seems, is different. Within minutes of sitting down on a busy street corner, an eccentric and well dressed man strikes up a conversation with you. He seems to empathise with your tale, and places a single gold coin in your cup before leaving. This coin is not the imperial standard, and you doubt that it would get past the eagled-eyed shopkeepers if you tried to buy something with it, but it is a beautiful looking coin nonetheless. It is engraved with markings that depict the sun circling the earth, truly a work of art, and with it being a gift from a sympathetic stranger you feel averse to parting with it. You give the coin a quick flip for good luck before pocketing it. For now though, it's time to try and earn some money.
Reach 10 coins.
1st of January in the year 600
Having finally scrounged up enough spare money for a few days worth of bread and water, you decide it's time to explore the town. Several of those who left you some coin were wearing a sect uniform, one of three kinds, and they each looked like they knew this town well. Thoughts of revenge flood your mind at the thought of these martial schools. With the power that they wield, a group of bandits would be nothing and you could take back your family home.
